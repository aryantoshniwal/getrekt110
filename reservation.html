Let's Power Your Day

EcoSpark

Our focus for this project is to create accessories that have charging capabilities without altering the appearance of the products that you use every day and love. We are currently thinking of accessories that will be able to charge your phone and smartwatch. This accesories are embedded into your everyday clothes ,so there will be no hassle of carrying an actual power bank. Our goal is to create even more accessories that are already commonly used in daily life but with our unique twist to assist everyday individuals. We are achieving this by utilizing the stages of design thinking.

1) Empathize - We empathized with the problem by conversing with our parents and others who travel daily for work. We discovered that people do not carry power banks as they require space and are quite heavy. Nonetheless, they need a way to charge their phone as they often forget to charge or run out of battery.

2) Design - We have designed a detachable power bank that will not only look stylish but also not diminish the belt by wires. We brainstormed this idea after discussing the matter with our teammates.

3) Ideate - We are currently in the ideation phase, where we are continuously coming up with new ideas and designs to make it even more user-friendly for our customers. We have thought of adding a small solar panel, which may enable the power bank to charge by itself while the person is outside. Our team is still coming up with fresh ideas like this to make our product stand out even more.
