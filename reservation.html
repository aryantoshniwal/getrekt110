Igniting Sustainable Celebrations

EcoSpark

We are focused on the Ecospark project, which aims to create eco-friendly firecrackers with reduced environmental impact. Our goal is to revolutionize the traditional firecracker industry by developing innovative and sustainable alternatives. Through design thinking, we are confident in our ability to create captivating firecrackers that emit fewer harmful chemicals and pollutants. Our mission is to make a positive contribution to environmental conservation while maintaining the excitement and joy associated with fireworks.

1) Empathize - We empathized with the problem by understanding the state of our surroundings that are getting contaminated by the means called firecrackers. We uncovered the fact that firecrackers are such big problem in the society especially during eventful occassions and such. This gave us the idea of solving and minimizing the use of firecrackers by introducing eco-friendly means and create awareness about it.

2) Define - The root problems caused by the firecracks are the ecological and economical imbalance that it produces which affects the mankind in a dreadful way. Firecrackers being one of the most economically-beneficial to the Government these problems go unnoticed and the public suffers from it. .

3) Ideate - We are currently in the ideation phase, where we are continuously coming up with new ideas and designs to make it even more eco-friendly for our customers. We have thought about producing such a firecracker which contributes less contamination to the environment thus leaving a lesser carbon footprint and also reducing the risk to mankind and wildlife.

4) Prototype - While we are still in the prototyping phase, we are diligently working on transforming our ideas into tangible representations. Although physical prototypes are not yet available, we are employing various visualizations and simulations to showcase our vision. These prototypes will provide you with a glimpse into the form and functionality of our eco-friendly fireworks concept.

5) Test - Your feedback is crucial in shaping the future of EcoSpark. We highly value your insights and opinions on our prototypes and ideas. We encourage you to share your thoughts, concerns, and suggestions regarding the feasibility, desirability, and potential environmental impact of our proposed eco-friendly alternatives. Together, we can refine and improve our solutions to meet your expectations.
